About Lüma

Hi, we’re Emma and Lucas, the couple behind Lüma.

Our story began long before this studio—it started with a chance meeting at a photography workshop in Paris. Emma was chasing natural light in the city’s vibrant streets, while Lucas was perfecting his craft in the controlled environment of the studio. Despite our different approaches to photography, we quickly realized we shared something deeper: a passion for capturing light and emotion in all its forms.
